Tiru

Wallet‑Based Digital Health Ecosystem
Unifying trusted healthcare provision and financial technology.

🌍 Vision

To build a unified, trusted, and financially inclusive digital health ecosystem that empowers patients, professionals, and institutions across Ethiopia – starting in Addis Ababa.

⚠️ The Problem fragmented & untrusted

📞 Phone‑dominated booking – inefficient, no real‑time sync with changing specialist schedules.
💰 No trust in fundraising – money raised for medical needs is often not locked for healthcare expenses.
📁 Poor medical records – disorganized, unstructured, leading to duplicate tests and no continuity of care.
🔍 Limited access to trusted providers – hard to find evidence‑based, reputable healthcare professionals.
💬 No private chat with trusted doctors – patients cannot get discreet, paid advice remotely.
🤖 No instant guidance – no AI or helpline to direct patients to the right care or consultation.
🆔 No unified ID – new registration at every healthcare facility, wasting time and risking errors.
📉 Poor proactive health savings – general population has no dedicated saving mechanism for medical expenses.
🏦 Distrust in traditional insurance – limited in‑network facilities, annual loss of unused premiums, complex claims.
📚 Lack of trusted local medical education – no accessible, Ethiopia‑based health information hub for patients.
🗺️ Information asymmetry – patients don’t know which health center offers which service or which specialist to visit.
📢 Doctors lack exposure – professionals struggle to attract patients, build reputation, or manage their brand.

✨ Solution: Tiru Platform

A wallet‑based, AI‑enabled digital health ecosystem connecting patients, providers, and institutions into one seamless platform.

🧑‍⚕️ Patients

  • Health‑only wallet (save + P2P support)
  • Digital directory & verified info
  • AI chatbot + helpline
  • Private consultations (subscription)
  • Universal Health ID (QR/code)
  • Organized medical records
  • Future microinsurance

👩‍⚕️ Health Professionals

  • Visibility & personal branding (broadcast, blog)
  • Appointment & seemless booking management
  • Income from private consultations
  • Continuous Medical Education (CME) and medical resources
  • Professional networking & case discussions
  • Recruitment via patient ratings

🏥 Healthcare Institutions

  • Efficient booking & patient flow
  • Universal ID‑based registry
  • Talent recruitment by ratings
  • Digital marketplace access
  • organised medical records upload to patient portal

📦 Pharma & Suppliers

  • Direct digital product exposure
  • Targeted marketing to institutions
  • Reduced reliance on field agents
